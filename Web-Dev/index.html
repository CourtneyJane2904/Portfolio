Hans Asperger

Asperger’s Syndrome -otherwise known as high-functioning Autism- is a developmental disorder identified by difficulties with social interaction, sensory difficulties and a unique world view. Although the condition causes it’s problems, it is also often found in individuals with high IQs and techies (e.g. Adrian Lamo and Gary McKinnon).

While my disorder means that I suffer with social anxiety and events out of my control, I also feel that coding logic comes naturally to me and I can grasp new concepts rapidly (if they interest me).

Adrian Lamo
February 20, 1981 - March 14, 2018

An American threat analyst and hacker, renown for the infiltration of several high-profile networks (Yahoo! and Microsoft are two examples).

In April, 2010 Lamo was institutionalized after his backpack holding his antidepressant medication was allegedly stolen and police noticed his monotone speech when responding to the incident; prior to this Lamo's father had reported his son's abuse of antidepressant medication.

After nine days in an institution, Lamos left with a diagnosis of Asperger's Disorder.

Gary McKinnon

McKinnon from Scottland is a systems administrator and hacker who gained his reputation in 2002 after hacking into military computers while searching for evidence of free energy suppression and cover ups of groundbreaking technology.

Over a 13-month period, McKinnon allegedly gained access to 97 US military and NASA computers under the name 'Solo'.

Though Solo claimed this was merely a search for evidence, he also deliberately brought down multiple critical military networks in the process.

During Solo's trial, an expert on Autism noticed Solo on television and immediately contacted Solo's solicitor.
